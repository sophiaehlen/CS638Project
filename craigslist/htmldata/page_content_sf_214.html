Perhaps the finest example of a true neo-classic influenced by bodywork evoking the luxurious timeless designs of the 1920s and '30s but with chassis and power trains from modern day automobiles, representing the culmination of many years experience of coach building and the passion of Mr. Ron Sparks, the Spartan II was one of the most successful neo-classics ever built. The Spartan II was hand built at it's own factory in San Marcos, CA, on a Nissan 300 ZX platform utilizing the luxury sport car components that were popular on the 300ZX cars such as:

High performance fuel injected Nissan 3.0L OHC V6 engine
4-speed automatic transmission
Digital gauges
Power steering
Power 4 wheel disc brakes
Power windows
Power door locks
Power mirrors
Heated Power Bucket seats w/Lumbar support drivers side
Climate control heat and A/C
T-tops w/Privacy Panels
Integrated alarm system
Authentic wire wheels w/ knock off hubs

Initially all Spartans were sold through Nissan [then Datsun] Dealerships. Approx. 300 Spartans & Spartan II's were built. Many were purchased by celebrity types such as Joan Collins, Eddie Murphy, Tony Dorsett, and Larry Holmes. It is a real attention magnet, and if you own one be prepared to tell the story,,,,forever.
